Wat Arun

Entrance: $3 per person

Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn, is one of Bangkok's most iconic landmarks located on the west bank of the Chao Phraya River. The temple is known for its intricate spires that stand majestically on the riverside.

Visiting Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Contact: +66 2 891 2185

Online Booking: No online booking available. Tickets can be purchased on-site.

The Grand Palace

Entrance: $15 per person

The Grand Palace is a complex of buildings at the heart of Bangkok, Thailand. The palace has been the official residence of the Kings of Siam since 1782. It is one of Thailand's most popular tourist destinations.

Visiting Hours: 8:30 AM - 3:30 PM
